Toddler

“I do it” sound familiar?

Toddlers like to take charge and learn through imitation and experimentation.

What may sound like the beginnings of a tantrum to you is actually a critical developmental milestone called ego formation where a child learns that they can master a skill through repeated effort. This helps form the building blocks of self-confidence and independence – and can help them achieve resilience, self-reliance and self esteem as they grow.
